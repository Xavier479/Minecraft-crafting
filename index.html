Minecraft Crafting Recipes

Select a recipe from the left to see how it's crafted!

Hint: If you're new to crafting, start with a crafting table it unlocks everything else!

oak planks

Place 1 wood

stick

Place 2 oak planks on the top of each other.

torch

Place 1 coal on top of 1 stick.

crafting table

Use 4 wooden planks arranged in a 2x2 pattern.

furnace

Use 8 cobblestones in a circle, leaving the center empty.

chest

Use 8 oak planks in a circle, leaving the center empty.

ladder

Use 7 sticks arranged like a H.

fence

Use 2 sticks and 4 wood planks. In a Crafting Table, place 2 wood planks in the first column, 2 sticks in the middle column, and 2 wood planks in the third column. Leave the bottom row empty.

boat

You need five wood planks from any wood type.

wooden slab

Place the planks in the crafting table's bottom row, with three planks in a row, to produce a wooden slab.

stone slab

Place the smooth stone in the bottom three slots of the crafting table to create six stone slabs.

sign

You need six planks of any wood type and one stick. Place the stick in the center and the planks around it.

door

Use six planks or six iron ingots and arrange them two wide and three high.

glowstone

Place four glowstone dust pieces in a 2x2 square within a crafting table.

snow block

Place 4 snowballs in a 2x2 square to craft a snow block.

TNT

Requires 4 sand blocks and 5 gunpowder. Place gunpowder in the corners and center, and fill the rest with sand.

clay block

Place 4 clay balls in a 2x2 grid in the crafting table.

brick block

Place 4 bricks in a square to craft a brick block.

bookshelf

Use 6 planks and 3 books. Place planks top and bottom, and books in the middle row.

sand block

Place four sand blocks in a 2x2 grid to create sandstone.

smooth sandstone

Place four sandstone blocks in a 2x2 square to make smooth sandstone.

decorative sandstone

Place 2 sandstone slabs vertically to craft decorative sandstone.

note block

Surround a redstone dust with 8 wood planks in the crafting grid.

jack-o-laterne

Combine a carved pumpkin with a torch underneath in the crafting grid.

lapis lazuli-block

Place 9 lapis lazuli in a 3x3 grid to make a block.

diamond block

Place 9 diamond ingots in the 3x3 crafting grid.

gold block

Place 9 gold ingots in the 3x3 crafting grid.

iron block

Place 9 iron ingots in the 3x3 crafting grid.

emerald blcok

Place 9 emerald ingots in the 3x3 crafting grid.

coal block

Place 9 coals in the 3x3 crafting grid.

stone brick

Place 4 stones in the 3x3 crafting grid.

wooden stairs

Use 6 oak planks arranged like stairs.

stone stairs

Use 6 stones arranged like stairs.

cobblestone wall

Place 6 cobblestones in the 3x3 crafting grid.

redstone blcok

Place 9 redstones (also called redstone dust) in the 3x3 crafting grid.

nether block

Place 4 nether ingots in the 2x2 grid.

quartz block

Place 4 nether quartz in the 3x3 crafting grid.

chiseled quartz block

place 2 quartz slabs in the 3x3 crafting grid.

pillar quartz block

Place 2 blocks of quartz in the 3x3 crafting grid.

stained clay court

Simply place the dye in the middle and the 8 hardened clay blocks around it in the crafting table.

hay bal

There should be 3 wheat in the first row, 3 wheat in the second row, and 3 wheat in the third row.

pickaxe

Using 2 sticks and 3 identical units of tool material.

axe

You need two sticks and then three planks, iron or gold ingots, cobblestone blocks or diamonds arranged in the top left corner of the crafting grid.

shovel

Place 1 tool material and 2 sticks in the 3x3 crafting grid.

sand block

Place four sand blocks in a 2x2 grid to create sandstone.

hoe

Place 2 tool material and 2 sticks in the 3x3 crafting grid.

decorative sandstone

Place 2 sandstone slabs vertically to craft decorative sandstone.

fishing rod

Place 3 sticks and 2 strings in the 3x3 crafting grid.

flint and steel

Place an iron ingot and flint piece on the craft grid.

compass

Place four iron ingots above, below and next to the redstone.

clock

Place the Redstone Dust in the center square. Surround it with the Gold Ingots in a diamond shape: One above. One below. One to the left. One to the right.

bucket

Place three iron ingotsarranged like a V.
